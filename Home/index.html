It was established in the year 2000 and is one of the best engineering and Management colleges in Eastern India. Computer Science & Engineering

The Computer Science and Engineering department of Dr. B.C. Roy Engineering College,Durgapur excels in academics, hands-on learning, and innovation. With NBA accreditation, our expert faculty, cutting-edge resources, and interdisciplinary approach prepare students for success in the field of computer science and engineering. Many graduates launch their own ventures or conduct groundbreaking research, reflecting our emphasis on entrepreneurship and community engagement. Moreover, the department consistently encourages the adoption and exploration of the latest technologies, ensuring our students stay at the forefront of advancements in the rapidly evolving world of computer science and engineering.
